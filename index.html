INNOVATEX HACKATHON

Welcome to InnovateX Hackathon — an exciting innovation challenge organized by the Computer Society of India (CSI SAHE) in collaboration with Google Developer Group (GDG VRSEC). This hackathon encourages open innovation and creative problem solving. Students will work in teams to build impactful solutions for real-world challenges and showcase their ideas through functional prototypes. Join us to innovate, collaborate, and turn your ideas into reality.

Hackathon Roadmap

1

Registration

Teams of 3–4 members must register and submit a PPT along with a demo link.

2

Evaluation (Round 1)

Submitted projects are reviewed based on innovation and feasibility.

3

Shortlisting

Selected teams receive an email invitation to attend the hackathon.

4

Technical (Round 2)

Judges evaluate the working prototype and technical implementation.

5

Presentation (Round 3)

Teams present their solution and demonstrate the working model.

6

Winners

Top 3 teams are selected and awarded prizes.
